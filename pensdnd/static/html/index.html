PensiveDND

A safe place for members of Pensive to play tabletop fantasy games together -- with roleplay and imagination, courage and deceit. The possibilities here are endless, and limited only by what your mind can dream up next.

But most-importantly, this website is not only meant to host a single game, but to be a vehicle for anyone in the guild to host games for other guildies, for free. See "Be a DM" for more details.
