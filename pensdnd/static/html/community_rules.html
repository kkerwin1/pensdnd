These rules apply to all game tables hosted at this site.

1. All guild rules are community rules. Therefore, all guild rules are rules at every table.

2. Roleplaying an evil character is not forbidden in the community, but dungeon masters may choose to set more strict rules than this. It is, however, officially strongly discouraged. There are a few ways in which it can work. There are a lot more ways in which it can fail. Further, ask yourself these questions, "Why is my evil character following this group of good characters around?" and "What is this group of good characters letting my evil character accompany them on their adventures?".

3. D&D is a cooperative game.

  • Do not act against the party.
  • This game is not about you. This game is about the party.
  • Be aware that most D&D party dynamics usually have one or more quiet people. Help the DM by inviting their participation in discussion, but respect their wish not to speak if they so indicate.
  • Be aware of everyone's strengths and weaknesses.
  • Be a team.
  • No drama will be tolerated

4. All DM rulings are law, even when they are wrong. (However, all DM's should be gracious in accepting correction, and try to do better in the the future).
